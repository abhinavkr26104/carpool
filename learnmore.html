What is goCorp

goCorp is a corporate car pooling service designed to help employees commute more efficiently, reduce traffic congestion, and decrease their carbon footprint. By connecting colleagues who live near each other or along similar routes, we make it easy to share rides to and from work.

Benefits of Car Pooling

  • Reduced environmental impact
  • Lower commuting costs
  • Improved workplace relationships
  • Reduced stress and commute time

How It Works

  1. Sign up for goCorp using your corporate email address.
  2. Enter your car details and usual commute schedule.
  3. Browse available rides or offer your own.
  4. Connect with colleagues and arrange your carpool.
  5. Enjoy your shared ride to work!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is goCorp only for employees with cars?
No, goCorp is for all employees. You can either offer rides if you have a car or join rides as a passenger.
How is the cost of the ride shared?
Ride costs are typically shared equally among all passengers, but the exact arrangement can be agreed upon by the participants.
What if my work schedule changes?
You can easily update your schedule in the goCorp website. We recommend communicating any changes to your carpool group as soon as possible.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and only shared with your matched carpool partners.
